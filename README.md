# PymeBoost

The current financial landscape in Costa Rica reveals a growing gap between individuals with available capital and small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in need of funding. While many people have accumulated savings, they often lack clear guidance and trustworthy channels to invest their money effectively. At the same time, SMEs struggle to access financing through traditional methods, which are often slow, restrictive, or inaccessible. This creates a paradox where capital exists but is not efficiently allocated, largely due to distrust, lack of transparency, and limited financial education.

This project proposes a platform that bridges this gap by enabling investment in specific, well-defined processes within SMEs rather than in the business as a whole. Instead of offering vague or high-risk investment opportunities, SMEs present targeted initiatives—such as improving marketing strategies, optimizing operational workflows, or enhancing service delivery—that directly impact their performance and growth. To ensure trust and reduce the risk of fraud, the platform verifies that each SME is properly registered in Costa Rica. Additionally, every investment opportunity is structured, monitored, and supported to provide clarity on expected outcomes and potential returns.

The platform acts as an intermediary that manages the full investment lifecycle, from validating SMEs and their proposed processes to facilitating transactions and ensuring accountability. Investments are executed directly within the application, where investors are informed of a projected return range. Furthermore, each process is expected to be implemented by a qualified specialist, ensuring that the proposed improvements are carried out effectively. While some operational aspects are still being refined, the core objective is to create a secure, transparent, and results-driven investment ecosystem that benefits both investors and SMEs by aligning financial returns with tangible business improvements.
